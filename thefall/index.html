Guest chef Bethany Zozula of Eleven will be joining her sister, Hilary Zozula of Eden to create a dinner event.They put their heads together and with the help of all the local farmers they came up with nine special courses.

Menu will feature

  1. 1st tomato, celeriac
  2. 2nd peach, watermelon
  3. 3rd radish, pickled plum
  4. 4th tartare: tuna and/or watermelon, avocado, sesame
  5. 5th ceviche: yellow fin and/or shiitake, cucumber, radish, jicama
  6. 6th carpaccio: lamb and beet, cashew, preserved lemon
  7. palette cleanser granita: basil, lime, ginger, honey
  8. 7th cheese: cashew, sheep, goat
  9. 8th apple, cashew, cinnamon
  10. 9th chocolate, avocado, blackberry

Sunday, September 22nd 2013

Dinner will starts promptly at 7:00pm

$55 per person.
